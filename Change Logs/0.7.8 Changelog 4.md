Two new Auction House features!  
## BIN Auctions
You can now create Auctions at a fixed price which instantly sells to the item buyer.  
![uE7rtUD.png](https://i.imgur.com/uE7rtUD.png&hash=d89bdb965f60498014966b1300d8f09c)  
Create a BIN using this button when you create an Auction  
You can buy BIN items from anywhere, but it will only auto-claim when in the hub:  
![GIWr9ZZ.png](https://i.imgur.com/GIWr9ZZ.png&hash=be5773485eba6ddd93b760a1d231e430)  
## Auctions Cancellation  
You may now cancel your auctions if they have 0 bids / haven't been purchased.  
![1T94dMX.png](https://i.imgur.com/1T94dMX.png&hash=90b70f82fd732a55bcd2b43d9ec61011)

1m to 10m: **5%**  
10m and more: **2.5%**
